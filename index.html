Our Story · 关于我们

A little apple
with a big personality

Xiao Pingguo — 小苹果, "little apple" — is our corner of Melbourne CBD where the ritual of the Japanese izakaya meets the warmth and punch of Malaysian home cooking. Born from a love of late-night dining culture across Asia, we opened our doors at 143–145 Bourke Street to bring that same convivial spirit to Melbourne.

Whether you're starting your evening with a round of drinks and shared plates, or wrapping up a big night with something satisfying, we're here until 1am — long after most kitchens have gone dark.

Our menu wanders freely across Japanese small plates, Malaysian staples and Asian-fusion creativity, all served in a space that's relaxed enough for a casual dinner and lively enough for the weekend crowd.
