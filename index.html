Our Story · 关于我们

A little apple
with a big personality

Xiao Pingguo — 小苹果, "little apple" — is our corner of Melbourne CBD where the ritual of the Japanese izakaya meets the warmth and punch of Malaysian home cooking.

We're known for our Lok Lok — the beloved Malaysian street food of skewered meats, seafood and vegetables — alongside authentic Chee Cheong Fun, pork belly enoki rolls, baby squid, lobster balls and a full bar to keep the night going.

Open from 8am for morning coffee through to 1am for late-night cravings. Walk in off Bourke Street and stay awhile.
